We connect leading Experts worldwide with Organizations in Africa to create and leverage great opportunties

Organisations in Africa

Are you a Public or Private Company, Institution, Start-up or Organization having a tough time sourcing for top talents?

Or are you specifically seeking Experts who can bring knowlegde and expertise gained from other climes to move your organization forward?

Here’s a way out!

PANDA opens you up to a pool of African and International Experts possessing a wide-range of technical and soft skills.

Experts

Are you an African or International Expert looking to relocate and work or share your expertise with Organizations in Africa?

Or do you travel on the Continent often and wish to link up with Africa-based Organizations or other Experts?

Or you just want to connect without travelling?

PANDA is your one stop platform for your networking needs.

PANDA's Missions

01

Networking

Link Africa-based Organizations with African and International Experts while serving as a hub for Experts to connect with other Experts.

02

Sharing

Promote a two-way value exchange (expertise and opportunities) between Africa-based Organizations and Experts.

03

Recruitment

Facilitate smooth recruitment and employment opportunity search for Africa-based Organizations, International and African experts by increasing the visibility of job offers on the Continent.

04

Collaboration

Aid collaboration opportunities in Africa.

05

Promotion

Create awareness for “Business in Africa” events.

Features

PROFILE DISCOVERY

Access the profiles and get in touch with Organizations in Africa or African and International Experts that aligns with your interest!

PUBLICATION

JPut your Organization’s job offers, events and announcements in front of a qualified audience. Experts can also attract publicity to their events and trawl through job offers within Africa.

TRAVEL MAP

Network easily by notifying Organizations and other Experts in Africa of your travel itinerary on the Continent.

EXTENDED FILTERS & SEARCH

Narrow down and discover search results that match your search intent fast by filtering based on criteria like activity, country and more.

NOTIFICATIONS

Get real-time alerts on job offers, events, travel schedule, and other publications from your favourite profiles!
